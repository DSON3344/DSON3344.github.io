About Me

Hello everyone! I am DSON, a multimedia design student passionate about creating engaging content. I specialize in video editing, UI/UX design, and have a keen interest in animation and game design.

I am active on multiple social platforms, including YouTube, Bilibili, Instagram, TikTok, Xiaohongshu (Rednote), and X (Twitter). YouTube and Bilibili are my main platforms for content creation. If you are also a fan of "Call of Duty Mobile," feel free to follow me and explore the endless possibilities of this game together!

My goal is to turn creativity into works that resonate with people and share my passion with the world. To learn more, check out my portfolio or connect with me through social media!
